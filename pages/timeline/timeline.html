Timeline

A brief timeline of Covid-19

  • December 31st, 2019

    Medical officials in Wuhan China announce a mysterious outbreak of an unknown illness.

  • January 7th, 2020

    The mystery virus was identified as a new strain of coronavirus. Symptoms were also clarified.

  • January 11th, 2020

    China reports its first Covid-19 related death, a 61 year old man from Wuhan.

  • January 21st, 2020

    The first confirmed case of Covid-19 in the United States of America is announced.

  • January 23rd, 2020

    Wuhan, China is officially placed under lockdown. Nobody is permitted to enter or leave Wuhan.

  • January 30th, 2020

    The WHO declares Covid-19 a global pandemic, as cases continue to rise globally.

  • February 8th, 2020

    The first American citizen dies from Covid-19.

  • February 11th, 2020

    The WHO officially recognize this new strain of coronavirus as Covid-19.

  • February 24th, 2020

    Fear continues to grow surrounding Covid-19, causing the stock market to crash.

  • March 8th, 2020

    Italy imposes strict lockdown measures, effectively quarantining millions of citizens.

  • February 24th, 2020

    Fear continues to grow surrounding Covid-19, causing the stock market to crash.

  • March 11th, 2020

    The WHO classifies Covid-19 as a pandemic.

  • March 11th, 2020

    The NBA suspends its regular season as a player has contracted Covid-19.

  • March 12th, 2020

    Many large organizations accross the world suspend operations as cases around the world continue to rise.

  • March 13th, 2020

    Schools and other businesses plan to close and shift to an online environment.

  • March 16th, 2020

    Canada closes its borders to non citizens.

  • March 16th-20th, 2020

    Events and organizations accross the globe continue to cancel or transition to an online environment.

  • March 20th-April 30th, 2020

    The world continues mass quarantines and lockdowns, with public fear and uncertainity at an all time high.

  • March 25th, 2020

    The WHO releases extensive information on social distancing guidelines and pleads for everyone around the world to wear N95 masks.

  • April 8th 2020

    Covid-19 vaccine trials begin in the USA.

  • April 13th, 2020

    The first saliva based Covid-19 test is approved for use.

  • April 25th, 2020

    The city of Wuhan claims to have eradicated the virus, and they have not had any new cases in quite some time.

  • May 1st-10th, 2020

    The world slowly begins to open up again, as people start returning to work and school in different nations.

  • May 15th, 2020

    China goes 1 month without any new Covid-19 deaths.

  • May 20th, 2020

    Global cases are announced to have surpassed 5 million.

  • May 27th, 2020

    The USA surpasses over 100,000 Covid-19 deaths.

  • June 10th, 2020

    Covid-19 cases in the US surpass 2 million.

  • June 24th, 2020

    Covid-19 vaccine trials begin in South Africa.

  • July 1st-7th, 2020

    Talks of a second wave begin to increase, countries continue to impose travel restrictions.

  • July 17th, 2020

    India surpasses 1 million cases of Covid-19.

  • August 10th, 2020

    The USA and Moderna strike a deal to supply 100 million doses of an experimental Covid-19 vaccine.

  • August 24th, 2020

    The first instance of someone being reinfected with Covid-19 is reported.

  • September 9th, 2020

    With children going back to school in America, over 500,000 children have been diagnosed with Covid-19.

  • October 2nd, 2020

    President Donald Trump is diagnosed with Covid-19.

  • November 16th, 2020

    Pfizer announces a new Covid-19 vaccine.
